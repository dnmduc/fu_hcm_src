​Authors' Guidelines:
  • ​All full papers must be composed in English and undergo evaluation with the stipulation that they are free from plagiarism
  • The maximum allowable length for a Full paper is 10 printed pages.
  • Detailed instructions for Full paper presentations can be found at: http://bit.ly/resfes_paper_presentation.
  • ​For those using LaTeX, a dedicated template is available at: https://bit.ly/latex_overleaf
  • All submissions of proposals and full papers should be channeled through the CMT system: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/ResFes2023.
  • Comprehensive guidelines on how to successfully submit your paper can be accessed here: http://bit.ly/CMT_submission.
  • When initiating the paper submission through CMT, authors hold the responsibility of ensuring precise alignment between the author list and paper title as presented in the submitted PDF file and the corresponding details on the CMT registration page.​
